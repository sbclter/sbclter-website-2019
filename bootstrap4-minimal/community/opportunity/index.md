<h3>Internship</h3>
<br>
<div style="position: bottom: 5px;">
     <p>Are you interested in gaining hands-on experience in real research projects under the guidance of passionate faculty and staff eager to serve as your mentors? If helping run lab experiments and participating in field research, including beach sampling and scuba diving, is something of interest to you, please fill out our <a href="{{site.baseurl}}/external/Documents/Other/Volunteer_Application_and_Waiver.pdf">SBC volunteer application form</a> now! We are in the process of returning to normal operations and are accepting new volunteers. We will be conducting the training in-person primarily, meetings via zoom may be scheduled if prospective internsâ€™ schedules conflict. If you have any questions about the application process, please do not hesitate to email <a href="mailto:scubaclint@ucsb.edu" target="_blank">Clint Nelson</a>. We encourage all motivated students to apply if you hope to gain invaluable hands-on experience in a marine science lab!</p>
